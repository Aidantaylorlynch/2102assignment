GAME OVER

The aim of this game is to get your score as high as possible.

If you hit asteroids you will die.

You can shoot as many lasers as you want.

Controls: Up arrow - Thrust, Left/Right - Steer, Spacebar - Shoot

features:

- score: as you shoot asteroids your score will increase

- svg: game objects are made with svg

- input: input is taken from the keyboard

- wrap: objects that fly off the screen (except lasers) will appear on the other side of the screen (torus topology)

- collisions: collisions between the palyer and asteroid will end the game. Collisions between lasers and asteroids will earn score and destroy asteroids

- thrust: the players ship will reach a maximum speed the longer you hold down the forward arrow. It will slow down overtime if you let go.
